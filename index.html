Navigate I: Intern/Grad Panel

The Intern and Grad Panel started our event series, offering attendees valuable insights from graduates, interns and recruiting managers at top firms like EY, KPMG and Orion Health.

Participants learned about the recruitment process and what it takes to stand out in today's competitive job market.

The event provided practical advice for navigating early career challenges: networking opportunities and how to do well in interviews/assessments.

Tickets No Longer Available

Navigate II: KPMG Firm Visit

Our Emerald sponsor, KPMG, allowed us to host a firm visit and gave students an exclusive look at the workings of a leading professional services firm.

Attendees toured the offices and learned about various career paths directly from KPMG professionals.

The event was an excellent opportunity for students to ask questions and build connections with recruiting managers and KPMG graduates.

It offered a unique insight into the corporate culture and career opportunities at KPMG.

Tickets No Longer Available

Quiz Night

Quiz Night was an evening of friendly competition where participants tackled questions across various categories.

The event fostered community spirit, bringing students together in a fun and engaging setting.

Teams competed for exciting prizes, making the night both challenging and rewarding.

It was a great way to network and relax while testing their knowledge in categories like Music and Geography.

Tickets No Longer Available

Diversity in Tech: Microsoft Experiences

This event, in collaboration with Rainbow Business and Microsoft, highlighted the importance of diversity in the tech industry.

Attendees learned about Microsoft's inclusive initiatives, such as GLEAM and 1K Queer, which support LGBTQIA+ students and professionals.

The discussion focused on the role of diversity in driving innovation and creating a more inclusive industry.

The event also provided networking opportunities and a deeper understanding of breaking down barriers in tech.

Our attendees got a Microsoft'sng in the office of one of the biggest tech companies in the world.

Tickets No Longer Available

EY Case Competition

We were privileged to host a case competition with Our Diamond sponsor, EY; the EY x BizTech Case Competition challenged students to solve real-world business problems in teams.

Participants joined teams and applied their knowledge to develop solutions simulating a high-pressure business environment.

The event highlighted the importance of creativity and analytical thinking in the workplace.

Out of the 16 teams, the top teams were invited to the EY office to present their ideas.

1st place was awarded to Case Mavericks, who were rewarded with an opportunity to interview at the assessment centre, giving them a competitive edge in their careers.

Tickets No Longer Available

DataEngine Fraud Detection Workshop

The Data Engine Workshop focused on fraud detection.

Attendees explored practical examples of how to build a fraud detection model.

The session was led by Indira Bandari, a Microsoft MVP, who shared her expertise and real-world experiences in the field.

As well as two other representatives from Data engine (Subashi Randeni and Serena Roncolato).

Participants gained valuable insights into the technical aspects and the ethical considerations of fraud detection in data science.

Tickets No Longer Available

Women In ISOM High Tea

Our most significant event for the year, which we are excited to host again, Is the Women in ISOM High Tea.

This event will empower female students to pursue their dreams in Information Systems and Operations Management.

This event will feature inspiring talks from women in the field, providing mentorship and networking opportunities.

Attendees will learn to navigate their careers and break barriers in a traditionally male-dominated industry.

The high tea setting will offer a relaxed environment to connect and learn more about what a career in ISOM has to offer.

We invite all women to attend and take advantage of your opportunities.

Tickets No Longer Available

Power BI: Case Competition

We are collaborating with the Data Science club to bring the Power BI Case Competition.

This case competition will challenge participants to uncover insights from four years of retail data.

Teams will compete for a $300 prize pool, applying data storytelling skills using Power BI.

This event is exclusively for DSC and BizTech members, encouraging teamwork and analytical thinking.

It's a fantastic opportunity to showcase their talents and work together to learn new skills, whether you are a beginner or an expert; in this case, comp is for all skill levels.

Navigate III: Leaders Panel

The upcoming Leaders Panel will feature accomplished professionals sharing their career journeys and leadership experiences.

Our final event from the Navigate series will provide insights into what it takes to succeed as a leader in today's business world.

It's an essential event for anyone looking to develop leadership skills and build connections, which may help you advance in your career in the future.
