FAQ

01 Who can join BizTech?

BizTech is open to all students, regardless of major or experience level! Whether you're into tech, business, or both, we welcome anyone with a passion for learning and innovation.

02 What kinds of events does BizTech host?

We host a variety of events, including industry networking sessions with recruiters, hands-on tech workshops, and collaborative innovation challenges. There’s always something for everyone!

03 How do I join BizTech?

Joining is easy! Just fill out our quick sign-up form on the google form linked in the nav bar, and you'll be added to our member list.

04 How can BizTech help with my career?

BizTech connects you with industry leaders and recruiters, providing valuable networking opportunities. Our workshops and events are designed to enhance your resume and prepare you for future career opportunities in both business and technology sectors.
